HOW IT WORKS!

Find the perfect recipe


Scroll through the world's largest recipe book and find exactly what your tongue desires!

We offer unique search filters that allow you to find the perfect recipes instantaneously! Search by meal, cuisine, holiday, taste, nutrition, diet, allergy, cook time, technique, and more.

Have your groceries delivered to your door


Find something you like? Take a look at the recipe and see if you're missing any groceries. Press "Grocery Me" and your groceries will be at your door in minutes, for the best priced
grocery deals in your neighbourhood!

Cook in the comfort of your own home


With all your groceries ready in your kitchen, follow the very interactive and intuitive recipe from the app! With handy features including measurement conversions, tips from the
chef who posted it, verbal recipe recitations, and more; your cooking experience will be stress-free!

Enjoy your creation


A great chef always tries his own creations! Enjoy your delicious new creation and save it in the Personal Favourites section of the app!

Help some fellow chefs out


Give the recipe a rating and leave some comments for your fellow chefs whether you liked it, loved it, disliked it, or tried it out with some slight alterations!

Add your favourite recipes


Post some of your famous family recips to the app for others to enjoy and love as much if not more than you do!

Take part in great benefits


Every time a recipe you post is used, you will gain Pantry Points! These points can then be redeemed for gift-cards to our sponser grocery stores.
